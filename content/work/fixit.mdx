## the problem

People report civic issues into a void. Reporting is fragmented,
untrackable, and opaque — a citizen who flags a pothole, a broken
streetlight, or a water leak rarely learns whether the report was verified,
acknowledged, or fixed. FixIt makes the whole lifecycle public and
accountable, with no government buy-in required.

## what i built

Two AI agents, not one chatbot. A reactive **Triage Agent** turns a short
conversation and a photo into a structured, geocoded, severity-rated,
de-duplicated record. A proactive **Watchtower Agent** runs on a schedule to
recompute urgency, cluster failures into Problem Zones with an AI root-cause
read, predict hotspots, and auto-write a weekly civic report.

## how it works

The Triage Agent is a Gemini function-calling tool loop that visibly reasons
across its tools — geocoding, duplicate-checking, live weather, and a
transparent severity score. The Watchtower Agent runs on Cloud Scheduler.
Every issue carries a public 0–100 Pressure Score that rises with neglect
and falls when authorities act, plus an immutable, timestamped “Issue DNA”
life-story.<FootnoteMark />

<Footnote>
  every AI and geocoding call runs server-side — the API keys never reach
  the browser.
</Footnote>

## results

A working, deployed platform: proximity-weighted community verification
(Reported → Verified), issue aging (Fresh → Neglected → Critical → Civic
Failure), and a login-free Public Impact Dashboard — all live on Cloud Run,
shipped as a multi-stage Docker container for the Vibe2Ship Hackathon
(Coding Ninjas × Google for Developers).
