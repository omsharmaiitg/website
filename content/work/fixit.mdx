## the problem

The same pothole gets reported five different ways, and none of the
reports add up to one issue anyone can act on.

## what i built

A civic reporting platform where citizens flag potholes, broken
streetlights, and garbage issues — and every report ends up as one
actionable issue on a live city map.

## how it works

An AI “Watchtower” agent built on Gemini 2.5 Flash classifies and
normalizes incoming reports and places them on the live city map.

## results

Built for the Vibe2Ship Hackathon (Coding Ninjas × Google for Developers)
and deployed on Google Cloud Run.
